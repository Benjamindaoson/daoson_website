我长期追问的几个问题 Research Agenda

问题一 question.one

智能体如何协作？ How do agents coordinate?

关注任务分工、上下文交接、工具调用和失败恢复，而不是单个智能体越做越大。 I focus on task boundaries, context handoff, tool use, and recovery instead of making one agent larger.

问题二 question.two

不可靠模型如何组成可靠系统？ How do unreliable models become reliable systems?

把评估、状态、回滚、人工确认和清晰边界当成系统设计的一部分。 Evaluation, state, rollback, human review, and clear boundaries are part of the system design.

问题三 question.three

构建者如何让学习持续复利？ How do builders compound learning?

用文章、笔记、今日所得和构建记录，把短期实验沉淀成长期判断。 Essays, notes, TILs, and build logs turn short experiments into long-term judgment.
